About our restaurant..

Kritunga Restaurant Private Limited is an unlisted private company incorporated on 29 June, 2010. It is classified as a private limited company and is located in Hyderabad, Telangana. It's authorized share capital is INR 25.00 lac and the total paid-up capital is INR 15.00 lac. The current status of Kritunga Restaurant Private Limited is - Active. The last reported AGM (Annual General Meeting) of Kritunga Restaurant Private Limited, per our records, was held on 29 November, 2021. Kritunga Restaurant Private Limited has two directors - Narender Kumar Reddy Tadi and Rajitha Tadi.
